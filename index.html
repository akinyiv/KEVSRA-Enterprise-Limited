About KEVSRA

It is a locally owned company that offers Engineering Construction and materials. It is dedicated to individuals seeking construction services.

Vision

To set the benchmark for quality performance by delivering solutions to our esteemed customers over ansd above their expectations.

Mission

We aim to be a world best engineering Construction Company providing efficient affordable, sustainable, cost effective services of highest level of quality

Our Services

Our area of expertise includes but not limited to building technology, civil and structural engineering.

Civil Engineering Structures and Roads Construction

KEVSRA offers a wide range of civil and structural engineering construction ranging from steel structures erections, reinforced concrete structures and road construction in gravel, paving blocks, concrete, bitumen roads and associated drainage structures.

Wall Branding

We do both indoor and outdoor wall branding services.Wall branding is done in shops, offices, houses and aslo events.Our strategies are unique and we work according to customers wants and needs to ensure that we achieve our customers goals by creating brand identity and awereness.

Tenders and Supplies

Our company bids for the supply of a specific project, product or service to the goverment, schools, hospitals, churches and other organisations.
